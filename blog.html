Essential Tax Deductions Every Small Business Should Know

Small business owners can significantly reduce their tax burden through various deductions. Key write-offs include common business expenses like office rent, employee salaries, and insurance premiums. Vehicle and travel expenses, including mileage and business trips, are also deductible. Home office deductions can cover portions of mortgage, utilities, and maintenance costs for dedicated workspace. Professional development investments, such as training and certifications, qualify for deductions. Technology expenses, including hardware, software, and cloud services, are also eligible. Proper documentation and separation of business and personal expenses are crucial for maximizing these tax benefits while maintaining compliance.
